PoneyaygyiRice

Description

Pone yay gyi salad is one of the famous burmese dishes. It is pretty simple to make.

It is a very popular dish in Burmese teashop and canteens.

Ingredients

  • Farmented Black Bean Paste
  • Chopped Onion
  • Fish Paste (Optional)
  • Sunflower Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add bean paste in the bowl
  2. Add fish sauce, sunflower oil and salt, one table spoon each
  3. Add chopped onion
  4. Mixed them well
  5. Ready to eat
